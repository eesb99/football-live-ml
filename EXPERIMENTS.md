# World Cup Prediction Dashboard Experiments

| Attempt | Description | Result | Evidence | Decision |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Live Poisson baseline | Existing API-Football live stats/events are converted to features and fed into a Poisson live model. | Works for in-play fixtures but cannot predict scheduled fixtures. | Existing tests pass for live probability invariants. | Keep as the live update layer. |
| Provider choice | Considered Opta/Sportradar/SportMonks versus API-Football for an individual project. | Enterprise providers are not practical for this phase. | User confirmed individual-builder context. | Keep API-Football only. |
| Elo pre-match layer | Add fallback local Elo ratings and Poisson expected goals for scheduled fixtures. | Works without historical data by falling back to 1500 ratings; improves when completed fixtures are available. | `tests/test_predictor.py` covers rating updates and pre-match probability invariants. | Keep for MVP. |
| Combined predictor | Blend pre-match prior with live Poisson output for in-play fixtures. | Produces live mode, next-goal outputs, and readable model drivers. | `tests/test_predictor.py` covers live blend behavior and red-card driver output. | Keep for MVP. |
| Fair benchmark | Score completed fixtures walk-forward before updating ratings, then compare API-Football only on shared fixtures. | Removes current-rating leakage from headline backtest and reports accuracy, Brier score, log loss, and API unavailable counts. | `tests/test_benchmark.py`, `tests/test_storage.py`, and `tests/test_app_helpers.py` cover walk-forward scoring, shared comparison, cache behavior, and metric math. | Keep as the model-vs-API-Football evaluation surface. |
| Conservative model improvement | Add probability-shape confidence, close-match draw calibration, prior-fixture form state, and miss/API-disagreement diagnostics. | Improves interpretability and avoids constant early-tournament confidence without training on a tiny sample. | `tests/test_predictor.py` covers confidence variation and draw normalization; `tests/test_benchmark.py` covers form leakage prevention and diagnostic counts. | Keep as transparent rules; do not train ML yet. |
| Draw diagnostics | Expose actual draws, draw misses, API draw misses, draw risk labels, expected-goal gap, total expected goals, rating/form gaps, and top-vs-draw margin. | Makes the observed draw-miss weakness auditable and refines draw calibration with general close-match gates. | `tests/test_benchmark.py` covers draw diagnostic rows, summaries, API draw detection, and risk labels; `tests/test_predictor.py` covers close-match-only draw boosting. | Keep as diagnostics plus conservative calibration, not sample-specific tuning. |
| SportMonks access audit | Add optional SportMonks token config, redacted client, World Cup All-in audit runner, fixture mapper, generated-data paths, and Provider Status tab. | Confirms current paid package access without changing model outputs. Latest live audit found World Cup league `732`, season `26618`, fixture `19606945`, fixtures/detail/xG/news accessible, predictions/odds/match-facts empty for selected future fixture, and no error categories. | `tests/test_config.py`, `tests/test_sportmonks_client.py`, `tests/test_sportmonks_audit.py`, `tests/test_sportmonks_mapping.py`, `tests/test_storage.py`, Streamlit AppTest, and `python3 -m src.sportmonks_audit`. | Keep as provider foundation; integrate scoring only after mapping and backtest rules are defined. |
| SportMonks cache and candidate benchmark | Cache World Cup 2026 fixtures, fixture detail, xG, and news; map API-Football fixtures to SportMonks; score a separate SportMonks candidate only when enrichment is provably pre-kickoff. | Gives practical coverage visibility without leaking post-match xG into pre-match scoring. Current headline model remains unchanged unless the candidate beats Brier score and log loss with enough eligible rows. | `tests/test_storage.py`, `tests/test_sportmonks_enrichment.py`, `tests/test_benchmark.py`, and `tests/test_app_helpers.py` cover sanitized caches, mapping coverage, non-leaky guards, and dashboard helper rows. | Keep as an evaluation lane; do not promote until eligible sample size and scoring improvement are proven. |
| Market odds and CLV gate | Capture SportMonks pre-kickoff odds, normalize full-time-result market odds into no-vig implied probabilities, compare model probability to market probability, track expected value and CLV, and block paper-trade flags until the benchmark gate passes. | Adds professional market diagnostics without turning raw edge into a betting recommendation. Current small-sample benchmark can still block all flags even when market data exists. | `tests/test_market_intelligence.py`, `tests/test_storage.py`, and Streamlit AppTest cover odds parsing, no-vig probabilities, benchmark gate behavior, edge flags, CLV deltas, and dashboard import safety. | Keep as research/paper-trade layer; do not automate staking or promote flags until Brier/log-loss and CLV evidence are strong. |
| Neutral venue and draw-risk calibration | Add World Cup host/neutral context, remove the standard home-advantage Elo boost for neutral World Cup fixtures, keep host advantage for Mexico/USA/Canada home venues, and lift draw probability for cold-start neutral close matches. | Directionally improves completed 2026 World Cup calibration: Brier `0.622 -> 0.614`, log loss `1.033 -> 1.020`, draw misses `3 -> 1`, accuracy unchanged at `4/8`; API-Football still leads at Brier `0.604` and log loss `0.953`. | `tests/test_predictor.py` covers neutral/host behavior; `tests/test_benchmark.py` covers walk-forward diagnostics; `python3 -m pytest tests` passed with 104 tests; Streamlit AppTest passed. | Keep as a bounded calibration improvement; benchmark gate remains blocked until sample size and Brier/log-loss evidence are stronger. |
| Cold-start neutral draw repair | Replace the hard `42%` cold-start neutral draw floor with a soft material-draw target that keeps draw competitive without automatically making it the top prediction. | On the current 12 completed fixtures, accuracy improved `5/12 -> 7/12`, Brier improved `0.620 -> 0.609`, log loss improved `1.023 -> 1.012`; API-Football remains ahead at `8/12`, Brier `0.611`, log loss `0.970`, so the benchmark gate remains blocked. | Current benchmark verifier reported reason `brier_log_loss_not_better`; `python3 -m pytest tests/test_predictor.py tests/test_benchmark.py tests/test_app_helpers.py` passed with 51 tests; `python3 -m pytest tests` passed with 105 tests; `python3 -m compileall src app tests` passed; Streamlit AppTest printed `streamlit_app_executed`. | Keep the repair because it reduces false draw top-picks and improves Brier, but do not promote market flags until log loss beats or clearly closes the API-Football gap. |
